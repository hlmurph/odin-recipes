Hummus

Description

A delicious dip made from Chick peas. Lemony, garlicy, and with a nice nutty flavor from tahini, which is made from processed seasame seeds, this dip goes great with pitas, chips, or spread on a sandwish or wrap.

Ingredients

  • 2 cans chick peas
  • 1/2 cup tahini
  • Juice of 2 lemons
  • 4 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 2 cups ice water

Steps

  1. Add tahini and lemon juice to blender, blend until smooth and lightened in color
  2. Add olive oil, salt, and cumin, microplane garlic into mixture, then blend until incorporated
  3. Add half the chick peas, then blend until smooth
  4. Add the rest of the chickpeas, then blend until smooth
  5. With the blender running on high, stream in ice water until desired consistency is reached
  6. Add seasoning, if desired (I like to add some Za'atar)
